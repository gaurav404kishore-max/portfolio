How it works
Each question is sent to the portfolio chatbot (Groq llama-3.3-70b). The response is then sent to a separate judge LLM call with a scoring rubric. Scores 8–10 = Pass, 5–7 = Partial, 1–4 = Fail.
Why this matters
Chatbots on personal portfolios commonly hallucinate or misrepresent the subject. These evals verify that "Ask Gaurav anything" responds accurately, stays in scope, and declines appropriately.
